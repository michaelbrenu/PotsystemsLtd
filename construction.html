Project Management

Controlled Delivery, Every Time

Our project management service provides clients with a single point of accountability for the delivery of their project — from initial programme setup through to final account and closeout.

We establish clear governance structures, define roles and responsibilities, and implement the controls needed to manage scope, cost, time, quality, and risk across the full project lifecycle. Whether we are acting as lead project manager, client-side PM, or providing PM support to an existing team, our approach is structured, transparent, and results-focused.

We work across building, infrastructure, refurbishment, and fit-out projects — applying the same disciplined controls regardless of scale or complexity.

PM Disciplines We Cover

  • Programme & Schedule Management — Master programme development, baseline setting, progress tracking, and critical path analysis to keep delivery on track.

  • Cost Management & Financial Control — Budgeting, cost planning, cash flow forecasting, valuation, and final account management with full financial transparency.

  • Risk & Change Management — Structured risk registers, mitigation planning, change control procedures, and impact assessments to protect project objectives.

  • Contract Administration — Procurement strategy, tender management, contract award, instructions, notices, and dispute avoidance throughout the works.

  • Stakeholder & Communication Management — Structured reporting, meeting management, decision logs, and stakeholder engagement plans tailored to each project.

  • Quality & HSE Oversight — Inspection and test plans, audit schedules, non-conformance management, and safety performance monitoring across the supply chain.

How We Engage

PM Engagement Models

Lead Project Manager

We act as the client's representative — taking full responsibility for programme, cost, risk, and quality controls from inception to handover. Suitable for clients without an in-house PM function.

Client-Side PM Support

We embed within the client's team to provide specialist PM support — reinforcing controls, filling gaps in capacity, and ensuring delivery disciplines are maintained throughout the project.

Independent PM Oversight

We provide independent assurance and review — verifying that programme, cost, and quality controls are functioning correctly. Ideal for funders, lenders, or boards requiring an objective view of delivery progress.
