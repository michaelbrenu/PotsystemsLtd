ARCHITECTURE & URBAN PLANNING

Sustainable design and urban master planning — shaping buildings and communities for the people who inhabit them.

Our Philosophy

Design With Purpose

We develop architectural and planning solutions that are functional, buildable and aligned to user needs, regulatory requirements and project budgets.

Our architecture and planning services cover concept development, space planning, schematic and detailed design, permits and approvals support, and interior design and fit-out. We work with clients to turn project ideas into coordinated design packages that support smooth procurement and delivery.

Specialisations

Architectural Expertise

Tropical Climate Design

Incorporating passive cooling and natural ventilation strategies that make buildings comfortable year-round without over-reliance on mechanical systems — ideal for Ghana's climate.

Timber Engineering

Combining environmental responsibility with modern building practices through engineered timber — a material that sequesters carbon while providing remarkable structural performance.

Concrete & Masonry

Time-tested structural materials used with precision and craft — delivering buildings with durability, thermal mass, and the robustness required by Ghana's environment.

Urban Planning Expertise

Urban Planning & Spatial Development

Urban planning and spatial development consultancy for international clients, backed by over 20 years of experience in Ghana, West Africa, Germany, and the Netherlands. Services include spatial development frameworks, structure plans, land use plans, and local planning strategies.

Urban Design & Inclusive Development

Urban design solutions focused on public space, inclusiveness, local development, and community-based planning. The approach responds to urban sprawl, informal growth, and the need for affordable housing, with experience rooted in Ghana and the wider region.

Climate-Resilient Planning & Knowledge Cooperation

Planning services based on climate resilience and sustainability. Concepts address flooding, heat, water management, green infrastructure, and sustainable land use, supported by university cooperation with TU Dortmund and KNUST.
